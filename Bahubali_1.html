Baahubli The Beginning

Here are some details about the 2015 Indian action film Baahubali: The Beginning: Director: S.S. Rajamouli Cast: Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, Tamannaah Bhatia, Ramya Krishnan, Sathyaraj, and Nassar Producers: Shobu Yarlagadda and Prasad Devineni Language: Filmed in Telugu and Tamil, dubbed into other languages including Hindi, Malayalam, and French Plot: The story of Sivudu, a young man who helps his love Avantika rescue Devasena, the former queen of Mahishmati Awards: 55 wins and 22 nominations Production: The film was shot with a lot of importance given to sound recording, with microphones embedded throughout the set to record ambient sound Production design: Sabu Cyril was the production designer, and everything was created from scrap Sequel: The film was followed by a sequel in 2017, called Baahubali 2: The Conclusion
