Dangal

Here are some details about the 2016 Hindi-language biographical sports drama film Dangal: Title: The title translates to "Wrestling Competition". Director: Nitesh Tiwari Producer: Aamir Khan, Kiran Rao, and Siddharth Roy Kapur Cast: Aamir Khan as Mahavir Singh Phogat, Zaira Wasim, Suhani Bhatnagar, Ritvik Sahore, and Girish Kulkarni Genre: Biography, drama, action Runtime: 2 hours and 41 minutes Release date: December 21, 2016 in theaters, June 22, 2017 for streaming Plot: The film is based on the true story of Mahavir Singh Phogat, a former wrestling champion who trains his daughters Geeta and Babita to become India's first world-class female wrestlers. Setting: The film takes place in the small village of Balali in the Bhiwani district of Haryana, India.
