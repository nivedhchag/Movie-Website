3 Idiots, Written and Directed by Rajkumar Hirani - The ...3 Idiots is a 2009 Hindi-language coming-of-age film about three engineering students at the Imperial College of Engineering (ICE) in India. The movie is about the happiness that comes from living for yourself. It stars Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, and Sharman Joshi, and was written and directed by Rajkumar Hirani. Here are some more details about the movie: The movie is rated PG-13 for thematic material, some disturbing images, and crude humor. The movie is based on Chetan Bhagat's Five Point Someone, but the screenplay and story are different. The movie includes the iconic dialogues "All is well" and "Jahapanah tussi great ho, tohfa kabool karo", which have become part of pop culture.
