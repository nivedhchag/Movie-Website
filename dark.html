The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight is a 2008 superhero film about Batman (Christian Bale) and his fight against the Joker (Heath Ledger) to save Gotham City from crime: Plot Set in Gotham City, the film follows Batman, Lieutenant James Gordon (Gary Oldman), and District Attorney Harvey Dent (Aaron Eckhart) as they try to put an end to the Joker's chaos. The Joker is a mysterious criminal mastermind who wreaks havoc on the city and threatens to kill Batman unless he reveals his identity. Themes The film explores themes of morality, human nature, and justice. Batman struggles to walk the line between heroism and vigilantism. Director Christopher Nolan directed the film, which was written by Jonathan and Christopher Nolan. Production The film was shot with IMAX cameras and features scenes such as a school bus crashing into a building, a rocket launcher being shot at an armored van, and a hospital being blown up.
