Sholay

Here are some details about the 1975 Hindi film Sholay: Plot: A retired police officer hires two small-time criminals to capture a notorious bandit. The film is set in the village of Rampur and stars Dharmendra as Veeru, Amitabh Bachchan as Jai, Sanjeev Kumar as Thakur Baldev Singh, Hema Malini as Basanti, and Jaya Bhaduri as Radha. Director: Ramesh Sippy Writer: Salim–Javed Producer: G. P. Sippy Music: R. D. Burman Cinematography: Dwarka Divecha Release date: August 15, 1975 Filming location: Ramanagara, Karnataka Running time: 204 minutes (original version), 198 minutes (theatrical version) Budget: ₹30 million Box office: ₹350 million (India), 250 million tickets (worldwide) Sholay is considered one of the most iconic films of all time. It was a huge success and held the record for the highest-grossing Indian film for nearly two decades. The film's songs and dialogue were so popular that they were released on audiocassettes.
