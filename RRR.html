RRR Movie Review: SS Rajamouli creates a Revolution with ...RRR (Rise, Roar, Revolt) is a 2022 Indian action-drama film that tells the fictional story of two Indian revolutionaries who fought against the British Raj and the Nizam of Hyderabad: Plot: The movie is set in the 1920s and follows the story of Komaram Bheem (N.T. Rama Rao Jr.), a family member who seeks vengeance against the British after a young girl is abducted and her mother is murdered. However, his quest is complicated by the fact that Alluri Sitarama Raju (Ram Charan), a fellow countryman, is working for the enemy. Themes: The movie explores the idea of what would have happened if the two revolutionaries had met and been inspired by each other. Cast: The movie also stars Alia Bhatt, Olivia Morris, and Ajay Devgn. Director: S.S. Rajamouli directed the movie, which was written by Rajamouli, Vijayendra Prasad, and Sai Madhav Burra. Release: RRR was released in theaters on March 25, 2022, and became available for streaming on May 22, 2022. Rating: RRR is rated R for strong bloody violence.
