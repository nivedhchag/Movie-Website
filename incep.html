Inception

Inception is a 2010 science fiction action movie about a thief who steals information from people's dreams and is offered a chance at redemption if he can plant an idea in someone's mind: Plot Dom Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio) is a professional thief who steals information from people's dreams. He's offered a chance to erase his criminal record if he can plant an idea in someone's mind. Cobb assembles a team to attempt the task, which includes Arthur (Joseph Gordon-Levitt), Eames (Tom Hardy), Yusuf (Dileep Rao), and Ariadne (Ellen Page). Themes The movie explores the idea of breaking into the subconscious to remove or insert ideas. It also explores the idea of reality and whether Cobb's reality is as real as it seems. Director Christopher Nolan wrote, directed, and produced the movie with his wife, Emma Thomas. Cast Also stars Ken Watanabe, Marion Cotillard, and Cillian Murphy. Reception The movie won four Oscars and was the fourth-highest-grossing film of 2010. Some say it's a creative and unique plot with intricate details, while others say it can be hard to follow.
